Legal Advice and Personalized Consultations

detaWe offer legal advice to people seeking information on immigration processes, answering questions, clarifying doubts and providing guidance on the necessary procedures.

Visa and Residence Permit Procedures

We help applicants understand the requirements for obtaining visas or residence permits. This includes preparing documents, submitting applications, and following procedures.

Renewal and Change of Immigration Status

We help people renew their visas or change their immigration status (for example, from tourist to temporary resident).

Family Reunification

We provide assistance in family reunification processes, which allow family members to join the holder of a visa or residence permit.

Nationality and Citizenship Procedures

We provide information on how to obtain nationality or citizenship in the United States.

Advice on Rights and Obligations

We provide information to migrants about their rights and responsibilities in the United States, including topics such as employment, health, education, and social security.
